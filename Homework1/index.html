Marge

Marge was created and designed by cartoonist Matt Greening while he was waiting in the lobby of James L. Brooks' office. Greening had been called to pitch a series of shorts based on Life in Hell but instead decided to create a new set of characters. He based the character on his mother Margaret Greening.
